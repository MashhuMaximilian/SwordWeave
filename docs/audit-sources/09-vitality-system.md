## 🧬 CORE IDEA

Vitality is the unified resource for:

- health
- stamina
- magical exertion
- capability strain

It represents **how long and how hard a character can push reality**.

---

## 🩸 BASE FORMULA

**Vitality = (10 + Proficiency Bonus) × Level**

This creates a predictable scaling curve across all characters.

(10 because mental + physical + magic must sum to 10 at character creation; each attribute ranges from −1 to +5)

---

## ⚖️ DESIGN INTENT

- Keep baseline survivability consistent
- Avoid stat inflation affecting HP directly
- Separate build (BU) from survivability

---

## 🧬 CAPABILITY BONUSES

Capabilities can modify vitality via:

- flat bonuses (+10, +20, etc.)
- special resilience traits
- situational buffers

These DO NOT change the base formula.

---

## 🛡️ DAMAGE MODEL

All damage is Vitality loss.

There is no separate HP/stamina/mana pool.

---

## 🧱 RESISTANCES

Instead of damage reduction systems:

Use:

- Resistance (reduce effective vitality loss)
- Immunity (negate specific effects entirely)

Examples:

- Fire Resistance → reduced vitality loss from fire effects
- Mind Immunity → ignores mental control effects

---

## 🎯 ENCOUNTER PRESSURE GUIDELINE

DM should tune encounters by intent:

- Easy: 10–20% vitality loss
- Standard: 25–50% vitality loss
- Hard: 50–75% vitality loss
- Boss: 70–100% pressure expected

---

## 🧠 DESIGN PRINCIPLE

Vitality is NOT just HP.

It is a **shared resource for survival + action expression**.
